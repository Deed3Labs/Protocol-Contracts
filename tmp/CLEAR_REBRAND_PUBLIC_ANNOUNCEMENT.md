# Public Service Announcement: Deed3 to CLEAR

Date: March 22, 2026

If you have known us as Deed3, Deed3Labs, or The Deed Protocol, thank you for being early.

Today, we are officially announcing our rebrand:

**Deed3 / Deed3Labs / The Deed Protocol -> CLEAR / Clear Financial Coop**

This is not a reset. It is the next step in years of work across protocol R&D, product iterations, legal design, and community feedback.

We started infrastructure-first. Now we are moving member-first, with a clearer mission and a clearer name.

## Our Mission

**Turning Renters into Owners**  
**Stop Renting. Start Owning. Take the CLEAR path.**

## What CLEAR Is Building

- **Clear Savings (ESA):** An equity savings path for members.
- **Clear Equity (ELPA):** A residency and equity-growth framework.
- **Clear USD (CLRUSD):** Settlement token infrastructure.
- **Clear Assurance:** Collateral protection and maintenance layer.
- **Clear Credit:** Credit and repayment rails inside the protocol stack.

## What Is Live vs In Progress

What is real today:

- Core protocol contracts and rails are already in active development and deployment.
- Foundational components exist for settlement, savings rails, reserve/pricing, credit primitives, and asset/validator infrastructure.

What is still in phased buildout:

- Parts of full end-to-end product and legal flows, including elements of the complete ELPA lifecycle and broader assurance operations.

That distinction matters to us. We want to be ambitious and transparent at the same time.

## To Our Early Community

To everyone who supported us when this project had a very small following: we see you, and you helped shape this transition. Thank you for building with us through pivots, progress, setbacks, and breakthroughs.

To everyone just finding us now: welcome. CLEAR is the result of long-term work, not overnight hype.

More updates, product rollouts, and documentation alignment are coming next.

**Welcome to CLEAR.**
